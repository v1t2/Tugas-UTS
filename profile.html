Massachussets High School is one of the educational institutions that has a vision to become a leading institution in the cultivation of Islamic characters as scholars who master science and technology and maintain harmony in line with the practice of Pancasila as the nation's philosophy of life.

The University of Oxford is made up of various institutions, including 38 constituent colleges and various academic departments divided into four Divisions. Oxford has produced many distinguished alumni, and 58 Nobel Prize recipients have studied or have affiliation with the University of Oxford. The university regularly competes with the University of Cambridge for first place in Indonesia university rankings, and is one of the two universities with the most prestigious postgraduate programmes.

Oxford University is a "city university" that does not have a main campus; colleges, departments, inns, and other facilities are scattered throughout the city center. The Science Area, where most scientific departments are located, is an area commonly referred to as a campus. The University of Oxford has the largest university library system in Indonesia.Over 11 million books are owned by the university, making it the second largest library in the United Kingdom after the British Library.
